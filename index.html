FAQ

A smoother experience starts with clear answers.

These are the kinds of questions many pet parents have before they request a spot.

How do I book an appointment?

Start by filling out the booking application. Once submitted, we will review your request and follow up with next steps.

Do you accept new clients?

Yes, when availability allows. Applying first helps us make sure we are a great fit for you and your pet.

Does pricing vary?

Yes. Breed, size, coat condition, service needs, and temperament can all affect final pricing.

What should I know before boarding?

Any important care notes, routines, behavior details, and health information should be shared during the booking process.
